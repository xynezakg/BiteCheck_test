BiteCheck Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 19, 2026

Welcome to BiteCheck, the canteen evaluation platform of the University of the Assumption (UA). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information required to ensure secure and verified canteen evaluations:

  • Official Email Address: We only authenticate users using their verified UA Google Account (ending in @ua.edu.ph).
  • Google Profile Data: We access your profile name to verify stakeholder identity.
  • Feedback Records: Ratings, stall selections, and written comments you submit.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Validate that ratings are submitted by authorized UA students, faculty, or staff.
  • Provide stall owners with analytical feedback to improve canteen menu and service quality.
  • Verify submission integrity using cryptographic receipt signatures.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information. Aggregated, anonymous feedback data is shared with canteen managers. Your email and name are strictly protected and only referenced by the system to prevent double-voting or malicious spam.

4. Data Security

BiteCheck uses modern industry standards to protect your data. Every feedback record is cryptographically signed at the source using Ed25519 signatures. If records are modified, the seal is broken and flagged instantly, ensuring total data integrity.

5. Account and Data Deletion

If you wish to delete your BiteCheck account and permanently remove all associated feedback data, ratings, and uploaded media from our systems, you may request deletion by:

  • Sending an email request to our administration team at canteen-admin@ua.edu.ph.
  • Upon receiving your request, your account profile, registered email index, and all uploaded evidence photos will be permanently deleted from our database within 15 business days. Feedback scores will be anonymized to maintain historical statistics.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the University Canteen Administration team or the IT Department.
